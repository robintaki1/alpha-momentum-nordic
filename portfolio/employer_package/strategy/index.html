The loop

What happens at month end

Read the cards left to right. They are the exact monthly actions, without technical labels.

1 Safety check

Is the market healthy?

If the broad market is below its 10-month average, do not open new positions. Stay in cash and wait for next month.

2 Score the stocks

Measure recent strength

For each eligible stock, compare the price from 13 months ago with the price from 1 month ago. Bigger rise means a higher score.

3 Build the basket

Keep the top 8

Buy the eight strongest names and give each the same weight so no single stock takes over the portfolio.

4 Repeat

Sell the dropouts

At the next monthly check, sell anything that no longer makes the top 8. Then start the loop again.

Plain-English takeaway: the strategy is a monthly leaderboard. If the market is healthy, keep the eight strongest eligible names. If not, wait in cash.

Green light or cash

The market switch decides whether the strategy can buy at all

Above the 10-month average

Rank the eligible stocks, buy the top 8, and keep moving.

Below the 10-month average

Do not buy. Hold cash and wait for the next monthly check.

  • Check the market first.
  • Only then rank the stocks.
  • If the switch is off, step aside.
Simple rule: the market filter acts like a safety switch. When it is off, the portfolio stays out of the way instead of forcing trades.
