Signal to trade

What happens each month

The strategy is intentionally simple. Most of the heavy lifting happens in the data and validation layers, not in the signal itself.

1. Score Find the strongest recent winners

For every eligible stock, compare the price from 12 months ago with the price from 1 month ago. That measures the trend while skipping the latest month.

2. Select Keep the top 8

Only the eight highest-ranked names make the portfolio, after tradability, capacity, and data checks have passed.

3. Buy Equal-weight the winners

The selected names each get the same weight, so no single stock dominates the book just because it is large.

4. Sell Exit the names that drop out

At the next rebalance, anything no longer in the top 8 is sold. If the trend filter is off, the strategy can stay in cash.

Plain-English takeaway: this is a monthly rotation into the strongest recent winners. The trend filter is the safety switch, and the sell rule is simply "anything that no longer ranks high enough gets removed."
