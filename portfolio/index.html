Operating model

Live vs paper vs historical replay

This portfolio is paper-trading in public. There is no live broker connection here; the current mode replays point-in-time historical data, prices the next available open, and applies friction before results are recorded.

Current state Paper trading active

No live broker feed. State updates monthly in papertrading/.

Live broker trading OFF

Nothing in this repo routes orders to a real brokerage account.

Paper trading ACTIVE

The frozen book rebalances monthly and writes its state to disk.

Historical replay IN USE

Signals come from point-in-time daily data, then execute on the next available open with friction included.

Trust checks: as-of availability checks, next-open pricing, one-way friction, and duplicate-month protection keep the run honest.
