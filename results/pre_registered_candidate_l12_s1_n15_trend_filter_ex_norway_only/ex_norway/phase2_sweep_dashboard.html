Protocol Insight

Why the first validation starts far back: Phase 2 is testing structural robustness first. If the idea only works in the latest regime, it usually should not survive to the later holdout or live discussion.

What counts as "after the test period" on this page: everything from 2018-01 onward is outside the Phase 2 selector and is meant to stay cleaner than the walk-forward folds shown below.
